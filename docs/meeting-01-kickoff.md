# Meeting 01 — Kickoff

Date: 2026-03-13
Project: Tarot Psychic Reader

## Purpose
Start the Tarot Psychic Reader project and establish the initial working model.

## What was decided
- Tarot Psychic Reader is an active shipping product under Pretty Products LLC.
- The top-level project folder is the clean, presentable version of the product.
- Department folders are the internal factory and can contain drafts, experiments, and rough planning.
- This was the first project meeting and serves as project kickoff.

## Working product idea
A tarot experience that feels magical, beautiful, emotionally resonant, and easy for regular people to use.

## Early product qualities
- pretty
- mystical
- emotionally engaging
- simple to start
- delightful rather than intimidating
- feels personal

## Open questions
- Is this mainly entertainment, reflection, guidance, or all three?
- What form should the first version take: chat, web app, mobile app, or simple landing page?
- Should readings feel theatrical and mystical, or warm and grounded?
- What makes this better than a generic AI fortune-teller?
- What is the minimum lovable version?

## Proposed first-track goals
1. Define the core user experience
2. Decide the first product format
3. Create initial brand and tone direction
4. Outline the reading flow
5. Identify legal/disclaimer needs

## Immediate next steps
- Product: define the one-sentence promise of the product
- Research: collect references and competing experiences
- Designer: define mood, aesthetic, and interaction feel
- Developer: propose lightweight v1 implementation options
- Legal: draft entertainment/disclaimer framing if needed

## Current status
Kickoff complete. Discovery and concept development next.
