# Meeting 01 — Product Skeleton

Date: 2026-03-13
Attendees: Holden, Sandy
Project: Tarot Reader

## Core decision
This project is actually a two-app product family.

## App 1
**Tarot Reader: Master**
- For tarot readers and apprentices
- Purpose: help users learn tarot reading

## App 2
**Tarot Reader**
- For everybody
- Purpose: read fortunes for regular users

## Reading modes in each app
Both apps should support two tarot reading modes:

### 1. In-app deck
- The app contains a tarot deck
- Users draw cards directly inside the app

### 2. Real-card scan
- Mobile app only
- Users use the camera to scan a real tarot card
